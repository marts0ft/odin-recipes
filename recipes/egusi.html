Odin Recipe

Description

Egusi soup is an african delicacy produced from melon seeds and infused with chopped vegetables and chopped meat. It's rich in protein since the broth from the meat is used to cook the soup. It's yellowish color is as a result of the palm oil on it. It's recommended to be taken with cassava flakes mixed with hot water to get the sizzling satisfaction.

Ingredients

  • Ground Melon Seeds
  • StockFish
  • Pumpkin Leaves
  • Palm oil
  • Local seasoning/Spices
  • salt
